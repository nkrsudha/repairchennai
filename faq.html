FAQs

Below is a list of Frequently Asked Questions

Why repair and not replace?

Because repairing will cost a fraction of original buying cost and with Mumbai Home Appliance Repair's guaranteed repairs, rest assured about the performance of your machine. And yes we don’t negate that you still have an option to buy a low quality cheap machine which is in the comparable price range of repair costs.

How can I trust your services?

Our vision is to become India’s top repair and maintenance service provider, both for household and industrial machines. To achieve this we want to grow into a premier brand by developing strong customer relationships and earning their trust and respect.

  • We are honest as we provide a free quote before undertaking any work
  • We respect your time as we offer a 50% discount on our service fee if our technician is late to come at your home
  • We are reliable as all our works are backed by 90 days guarantee
  • We are fast as we provide quick turnaround time with express service option
  • We are convenient as we provide multiple service request through which you can raise your request 24X7
What Appliance Models do you service?

We service all makes and models.

What is the approximate break-up of your charges?

After inspection, a quote is provided to you. Owing to the variations in services offered across different cities, it is not possible for us to share the cost upfront, without one of our service provider inspecting the device first.

Do you provide material and/or service warranty?

Warranty or guarantee on parts are offered as described on the material’s bill. Please insist on a material bill from the service provider, in case of external material charges. Regarding service, there is a 30 days warranty, such that if a problem of the same nature reoccurs, our services will be free of cost
